Gaussian Prior on Beta

Input and Assumption Nodes

Inputs are param.beta and param.tau. The prior assumes the existing coefficient vector has Gaussian distribution with known positive scale.

Declaration

The prior is

\[ \beta \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\tau^2 I_d). \]

Result

The node prior.gaussian_beta supplies the quadratic prior penalty used by the posterior and MAP objective.
