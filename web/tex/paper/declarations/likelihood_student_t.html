Student-t Likelihood

Input and Assumption Nodes

Input node: model.student_t_noise. The scale \(\sigma\) and degrees of freedom \(\nu\) are known.

Declaration

The likelihood is the product of Student-t densities over residuals \(r_i=y_i-x_i^\top\beta\):

\[ p(y\mid X,\beta,\sigma,\nu) = \prod_{i=1}^n \frac{\Gamma((\nu+1)/2)}{\Gamma(\nu/2)\sqrt{\nu\pi}\sigma} \left(1+\frac{r_i^2}{\nu\sigma^2}\right)^{-(\nu+1)/2}. \]

Result

The node likelihood.student_t defines the robust likelihood used by the Student-t MAP approximation.
