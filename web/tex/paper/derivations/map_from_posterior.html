MAP from Gaussian Posterior

Input and Assumption Nodes

Input node: posterior.gaussian_conjugate.

Derivation

For a Gaussian posterior \(p(\beta\mid X,y)=\mathcal{N}(\mu_n,\Sigma_n)\), the density is maximized at its mean:

\[ \hat{\beta}_{MAP} = \mu_n. \]

Result

The MAP estimator equals the posterior mean in estimator.beta_map.
