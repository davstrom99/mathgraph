\section{Gaussian Posterior Derivation}
\label{deriv:gaussian-posterior}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input nodes: \texttt{likelihood.gaussian} and \texttt{prior.gaussian\_beta}.

\subsection{Derivation}
Combining likelihood and prior gives the exponent
\[
  -\frac{1}{2\sigma^2}\|y-X\beta\|_2^2
  -
  \frac{1}{2\tau^2}\|\beta\|_2^2.
\]
Completing the square yields
\[
  \Sigma_n =
  \left(\frac{1}{\sigma^2}X^\top X+\frac{1}{\tau^2}I_d\right)^{-1},
  \qquad
  \mu_n = \Sigma_n\frac{1}{\sigma^2}X^\top y.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The posterior node \texttt{posterior.gaussian\_conjugate} returns \(p(\beta\mid X,y)=\mathcal{N}(\mu_n,\Sigma_n)\).
