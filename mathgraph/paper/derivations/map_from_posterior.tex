\section{MAP from Gaussian Posterior}
\label{deriv:map-from-posterior}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{posterior.gaussian\_conjugate}.

\subsection{Derivation}
For a Gaussian posterior \(p(\beta\mid X,y)=\mathcal{N}(\mu_n,\Sigma_n)\), the density is maximized at its mean:
\[
  \hat{\beta}_{MAP} = \mu_n.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The MAP estimator equals the posterior mean in \texttt{estimator.beta\_map}.
