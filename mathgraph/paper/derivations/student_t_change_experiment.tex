\section{Student-t Change Experiment Derivation}
\label{deriv:student-t-change-experiment}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{approx.student\_t\_map}.

\subsection{Derivation}
The experiment compares coefficient recovery under Gaussian MAP and Student-t MAP when data are generated with heavy-tailed residuals:
\[
  \|\widehat{\beta}_{G}-\beta_0\|_2
  \quad\text{versus}\quad
  \|\widehat{\beta}_{t}-\beta_0\|_2.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The experiment will generate \texttt{results/student\_t\_change\_summary.json} and \texttt{results/student\_t\_change\_error.png}.
