\section{Beta to Gaussian Model}
\label{edge:beta-to-gaussian-model}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{param.beta}. Result node: \texttt{model.gaussian\_observation}.

\subsection{Derivation}
The mean of the observation model is \(X\beta\), so \(\beta\) is the unknown coefficient vector in the linear predictor.

\subsection{Result}
The parameter \(\beta\) is required by the Gaussian observation model.
