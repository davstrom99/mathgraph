\section{Degrees of Freedom to Student-t Model}
\label{edge:nu-to-student-t-model}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{param.nu}. Result node: \texttt{model.student\_t\_noise}.

\subsection{Derivation}
The Student-t observation model uses the positive degrees of freedom \(\nu\) in
\[
  \epsilon_i \sim t_\nu(0,\sigma).
\]

\subsection{Result}
The parameter \(\nu\) controls the tail heaviness of the Student-t noise distribution.
