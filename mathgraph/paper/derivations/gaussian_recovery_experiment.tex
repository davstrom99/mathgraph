\section{Gaussian Recovery Experiment Derivation}
\label{experiment:gaussian-recovery}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{validation.synthetic\_recovery}.

\subsection{Derivation}
Run the validation over \(n\in\{20,50,100,250,500\}\) and fixed random seeds, then summarize the empirical mean and standard deviation of \(e_n\).

The experiment generates \texttt{results/gaussian\_recovery\_summary.json} and \texttt{results/gaussian\_recovery\_error.png}.
