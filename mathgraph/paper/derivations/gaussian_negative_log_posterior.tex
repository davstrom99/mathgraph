\section{Gaussian Negative Log Posterior Derivation}
\label{deriv:gaussian-negative-log-posterior}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{posterior.gaussian\_conjugate}.

\subsection{Derivation}
Dropping constants independent of \(\beta\), the negative log posterior is
\[
  \frac{1}{2\sigma^2}\|y-X\beta\|_2^2
  +
  \frac{1}{2\tau^2}\|\beta\|_2^2.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The result node is \texttt{objective.gaussian\_negative\_log\_posterior}.
