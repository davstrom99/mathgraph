\section*{Deterministic products of the evidence index}
For authoritative index records \(I_A\), dossier, reference, and validation outputs are deterministic projections:
\begin{equation}
(D,R,V)=\bigl(\operatorname{compact}(I_A),\operatorname{occurrences}(I_A),\operatorname{check}(I)\bigr).
\label{deriv:init-index-products}
\end{equation}
Thus the same indexed evidence supports compact review, exhaustive exact-line drafting, and integrity validation without repeated semantic transcription.
