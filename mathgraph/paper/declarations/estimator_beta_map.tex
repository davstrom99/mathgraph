\section{MAP Estimator for Beta}
\label{estimator:beta-map}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input nodes: \texttt{objective.gaussian\_negative\_log\_posterior} and \texttt{posterior.gaussian\_conjugate}.

\subsection{Declaration}
The estimator is
\[
  \hat{\beta}_{MAP}
  =
  \arg\min_\beta
  \frac{1}{2\sigma^2}\|y-X\beta\|_2^2
  +
  \frac{1}{2\tau^2}\|\beta\|_2^2.
\]

\subsection{Result}
For the Gaussian conjugate model, the MAP estimator equals the posterior mean \(\mu_n\).
