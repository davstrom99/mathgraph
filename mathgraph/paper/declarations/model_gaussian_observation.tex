\section{Gaussian Observation Model}
\label{model:gaussian-observation}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Inputs are \texttt{var.X}, \texttt{var.y}, \texttt{param.beta}, \texttt{var.epsilon}, and \texttt{param.sigma}. The model assumes iid Gaussian observation noise.

\subsection{Declaration}
The observation model is
\[
  y = X\beta + \epsilon,
  \qquad
  \epsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\sigma^2 I_n).
\]
The implementation samples rows of \(X\) from a standard normal distribution and then forms \(y=X\beta+\epsilon\).

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{model.gaussian\_observation} defines the data-generating Gaussian linear model.
