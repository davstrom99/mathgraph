\section{Student-t Likelihood}
\label{likelihood:student-t}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{model.student\_t\_noise}. The scale \(\sigma\) and degrees of freedom \(\nu\) are known.

\subsection{Declaration}
The likelihood is the product of Student-t densities over residuals \(r_i=y_i-x_i^\top\beta\):
\[
  p(y\mid X,\beta,\sigma,\nu)
  =
  \prod_{i=1}^n
  \frac{\Gamma((\nu+1)/2)}{\Gamma(\nu/2)\sqrt{\nu\pi}\sigma}
  \left(1+\frac{r_i^2}{\nu\sigma^2}\right)^{-(\nu+1)/2}.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{likelihood.student\_t} defines the robust likelihood used by the Student-t MAP approximation.
