\section{Closed-Form Gaussian Posterior}
\label{posterior:gaussian-conjugate}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input nodes: \texttt{likelihood.gaussian} and \texttt{prior.gaussian\_beta}.

\subsection{Declaration}
The posterior is Gaussian:
\[
  p(\beta\mid X,y)=\mathcal{N}(\mu_n,\Sigma_n).
\]

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{posterior.gaussian\_conjugate} returns \((\mu_n,\Sigma_n)\).
