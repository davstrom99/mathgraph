\section{Gaussian Prior on Beta}
\label{prior:gaussian-beta}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Inputs are \texttt{param.beta} and \texttt{param.tau}. The prior assumes the existing coefficient vector has Gaussian distribution with known positive scale.

\subsection{Declaration}
The prior is
\[
  \beta \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\tau^2 I_d).
\]

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{prior.gaussian\_beta} supplies the quadratic prior penalty used by the posterior and MAP objective.
