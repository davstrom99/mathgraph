\section{Student-t Degrees of Freedom Declaration}
\label{def:nu}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
No upstream mathematical node is required. This declaration introduces the Student-t degrees-of-freedom node \texttt{param.nu}.

\subsection{Declaration}
The degrees of freedom parameter is
\[
  \nu \in \mathbb{R}_{>0}.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{param.nu} supplies the tail-heaviness parameter used by the Student-t noise model.
