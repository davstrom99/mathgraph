\section{Gaussian Negative Log Posterior}
\label{objective:gaussian-negative-log-posterior}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{posterior.gaussian\_conjugate}. Constants independent of \(\beta\) are omitted.

\subsection{Declaration}
The objective is
\[
  \frac{1}{2\sigma^2}\|y-X\beta\|_2^2
  +
  \frac{1}{2\tau^2}\|\beta\|_2^2.
\]

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{objective.gaussian\_negative\_log\_posterior} is the scalar objective minimized by the Gaussian MAP estimator.
