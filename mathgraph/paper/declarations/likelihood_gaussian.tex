\section{Gaussian Likelihood}
\label{likelihood:gaussian}

\subsection{Input and Assumption Nodes}
Input node: \texttt{model.gaussian\_observation}. The variance \(\sigma^2\) is known.

\subsection{Declaration}
The Gaussian likelihood is
\[
  p(y\mid X,\beta,\sigma^2)
  =
  (2\pi\sigma^2)^{-n/2}
  \exp\left(-\frac{1}{2\sigma^2}\|y-X\beta\|_2^2\right).
\]

\subsection{Result}
The node \texttt{likelihood.gaussian} evaluates the log likelihood for fixed \(X,y,\beta,\sigma\).
